Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to LearnLens! This Privacy Policy explains how LearnLens ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our website (https://learnlens.onrender.com/) and services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This policy applies to all users of LearnLens, including quiz creators and participants.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you register for a LearnLens account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account preferences and settings

2. Social Login Information

If you choose to register or log in through Google or Facebook, we may receive:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Other information you've made publicly available on these platforms

3. Quiz Data

  • Quiz content created by users
  • Quiz responses and results
  • Completion time and progress data

4. Facial Analysis Data

With your consent, we collect:

  • Facial expression data during quiz sessions
  • Emotional response classifications (satisfied, unsatisfied, or neutral)
  • Timestamps associated with facial data

5. Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Time zone and location information
  • Click patterns and usage data
  • Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

Account Management

  • To create and maintain your LearnLens account
  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

Platform Functionality

  • To enable quiz creation, participation, and scoring
  • To analyze facial expressions during quiz sessions
  • To provide emotional feedback and insights on quiz engagement
  • To improve quiz content based on user responses and emotional data

Platform Improvement

  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • To fix bugs and troubleshoot platform issues
  • To conduct research and development

Communication

  • To send important notices and updates about the platform
  • To provide information about features, updates, or changes
  • To send marketing communications (with opt-out options)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and we implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

Facial analysis data is processed using industry-standard encryption protocols. While we make every effort to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services. Facial analysis data is stored for 90 days after collection, after which it is aggregated and anonymized for analytical purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Sharing Your Information

Service Providers

We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Payment processors
  • Customer support tools

These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for providing services to us and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, government requests).

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Your Rights and Choices

Account Information

You can access, update, or delete your account information by logging into your account settings or contacting us directly.

Facial Analysis Consent

Participation in facial analysis is optional. You can:

  • Opt out of facial analysis features in your account settings
  • Revoke consent at any time
  • Request deletion of previously collected facial data

Communication Preferences

You can manage your communication preferences by:

  • Updating your notification settings in your account
  • Using the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Contacting us directly

Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our platform.

Children's Privacy

LearnLens is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our systems.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your information receives adequate protection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
